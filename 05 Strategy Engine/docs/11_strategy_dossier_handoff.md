# 11 Strategy Dossier Handoff

## Purpose

The Strategy Dossier is the external handoff artifact from 05 Strategy Engine to 06 Backtesting Engine.

## Closure vs Dossier

Strategy Closure verifies that the candidate is internally complete.

Strategy Dossier Handoff packages the candidate for downstream evaluation by 06.

## Dossier Contents

A future dossier should include:

- Candidate identity and status.
- Source artifact references.
- Signal definitions.
- Regime assumptions.
- Rule framing.
- Risk template.
- Quality gate results.
- Falsification criteria.
- Known limitations.
- Test questions for 06.

## Explicit Non-Scope

The dossier does not include backtest results, PnL, execution reports, live deployment configuration, or profitability claims.
