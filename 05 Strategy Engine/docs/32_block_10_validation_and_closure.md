# 32 Block 10 Validation And Closure

## Scope Completed

Block 10 creates Strategy Closure as an internal governance completion layer.

It defines:

- closure record structure;
- internal closure status;
- required positive quality assessment origin;
- traceability preservation;
- upstream falsification reference preservation;
- synthetic mockups;
- unit tests.

## Validation Intent

Validation proves only that a conceptual package with positive Quality Gates can receive an internal Strategy Engine closure record.

It does not create dossier handoff, authorize backtesting, validate performance, confirm edge, or authorize trading.

## Expected Test Coverage

The Block 10 tests must show that:

- a valid closure record can be created from a passing quality assessment;
- non-assessment origins are rejected;
- failed or revision-required quality assessments are rejected;
- manual traceability mismatches are rejected;
- assumptions, limitations, non-approval statement, and audit reference are enforced;
- upstream traceability and falsification references are preserved;
- closure records are immutable;
- unsupported closure status is rejected;
- closure records do not expose dossier, handoff, backtest, PnL, performance, execution, or trading fields.

## Closure Conditions

Block 10 is closed when:

- the Strategy Closure contract is documented;
- implementation is covered by tests;
- closure is constrained to positive quality assessments;
- closure status remains `closed_pending_dossier_handoff`;
- no real candidates are closed from real data;
- no dossier, handoff, backtesting, PnL, performance metrics, execution, or data integration are created;
- 04 Research Layer is not modified.

## Non-Approval Statement

No closure record created under this block confirms edge, profitability, safety, empirical robustness, tradability, deployability, backtest readiness, dossier handoff, or execution readiness.
