# 06 Strategy Composition Layer

## Purpose

The Strategy Composition Layer defines how abstract signals, rule frames, filters, regime context, and risk templates are combined into a coherent strategy candidate.

## Composition Inputs

Composition may reference:

- Governed upstream research artifacts.
- Abstract signal definitions.
- Regime context framing.
- Rule construction notes.
- Risk template constraints.
- Quality gate requirements.

## Explicit Non-Scope

Strategy composition does not perform backtesting, profitability validation, live simulation, PnL calculation, or execution design.

## Output

The output is a structured strategy candidate ready for registry review and later dossier handoff, not a validated trading strategy.
