# Reports

This directory contains report packages and dossier artifacts produced by 04 Research Layer.

Reports here may include:

- base dossier templates;
- methodology summaries;
- future feature intelligence reports;
- future hypothesis candidate packages;
- future findings registry exports;
- future anti-overfitting certification artifacts.

Reports here must not include:

- invented findings;
- auto-generated hypotheses presented as human-reviewed;
- trading signals;
- strategy rules;
- backtest results unless explicitly produced by a later authorized layer;
- claims of edge without quality-reviewed evidence.

Placeholders are allowed when clearly labeled. A placeholder is not a research result.
