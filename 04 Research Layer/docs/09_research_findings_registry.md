# Research Findings Registry

## Concept

The Research Findings Registry stores structured findings derived from quantitative analysis.

Findings must follow the chain:

`data cuantitativos -> metricas -> reglas de interpretacion -> findings estructurados`

## Required Finding Fields

Each finding should eventually include:

- Finding identifier.
- Related hypothesis or analysis.
- Feature or feature group.
- Asset and timeframe scope.
- Evidence summary.
- Metrics.
- Score.
- Risks.
- Caveats.
- Decision.
- Reproducibility references.

## Allowed Decisions

Finding decisions are:

- Advance to 05 Strategy Engine.
- Defer.
- Reject.
- Needs more data.

## Boundary

Findings cannot be invented by an LLM. They must come from quantitative evidence and predefined interpretation rules. No finding is created in Block 1.
